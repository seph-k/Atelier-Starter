About Us

A decade of service

Chi Chi's hair salon was founded in 2012 by Cheryl. Our salon offers high-quality hair extensions, bridal hairstyles, and functional hairstyles. Having been in the hair industry for more than 10 years, Chi Chi's has a wealth of experience to draw upon. There is always a great deal of activity in Chi Chi's salon. Using only the industry's finest products. With our creative and dedicated team, we strive to create a relaxing and fun environment for our clients to enjoy.
